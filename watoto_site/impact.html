Impact & Achievements

Our Impact

Watoto Are Tomorrow has made a significant impact on the lives of refugee children and their communities. We have reached over 3,000 children, created 50 jobs, and established multiple learning hubs.

  • 3,000+ children reached
  • 50 jobs created
  • Multiple learning hubs established

Our Achievements

We are proud of our achievements, which include receiving awards, grants, and reaching significant milestones. Our work has been recognized by various organizations and has made a lasting impact on the communities we serve.
